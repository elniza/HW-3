Moon Lovers

By famous dorama

Moonlight Lovers - Scarlet Heart: Goryeo is a 2016 South Korean television series based on the Chinese novel Everywhere Striking but set to the Korean historical period of Goryeo. It aired from August 29 to November 1, 2016 on SBS every Monday and Tuesday at 10:00 pm (KST).

Plot

By famous dorama

During a solar eclipse, a girl from the 21st century, Go Ha-jin, is transported to the days of the kingdom of Goryeo. She wakes up in 941 in Hae Soo's body during the reign of King Taejo. As the story progresses, Hae Soo finds herself unwittingly embroiled in palace intrigue and rivalry between princes vying for the throne.

Cast members

By famous dorama

Leading Roles: Lee Joon Gi as 4th Prince Wang So, Lee Ji Eun - Go Ha Jin / Hae Soo, Kang Ha Neul - 8th Prince Wang Wook.

Perception

By famous dorama

On October 28, 2016, the drama won the K-Culture Pride Award at the Korea Brand Awards for Cross-Cultural Exchange of Korea and China, and at the end of the year, the drama was nominated for twelve nominations at the SBS Drama Awards, winning a total of seven. “Moon Lovers” was named the most talked about drama of the year by the research institute Good Data Corporation after “Descendants of the Sun” and “Moonlight Drawn by Clouds.”
