The K2

By famous dorama

The K2 is a 2016 South Korean television series starring Ji Chang Wook and Im Yun-ah. 16 episodes premiere on tvN every Friday and Saturday at 8:00 pm from September 23 to November 12, 2016.

Plot

By famous dorama

Kim Jae-ha is a former mercenary soldier from Blackstone PMC. While in Iraq, he is accused of killing his lover Rania, a civilian. As a result, he runs away and becomes a fugitive. He returns to South Korea and accidentally receives an offer to work as a bodyguard from Choi Yoo-jin, owner of the JSS Security Company and wife of presidential candidate Jang Se-jun.

Plot

By famous dorama

He accepts the job in exchange for the resources he needs to get revenge on another presidential candidate, Park Kwang-soo, who had previously ordered the murder of his lover. Meanwhile, Jae-ha is assigned to guard Go An-na, Jang Se-joon's hidden daughter, whose life is always in danger because of Yoo-jin, her stepmother.

An-na, who has been reclusive and lonely all her life, begins to rely on Je-ha, who takes care of her and protects her at all costs. They gradually fall in love with each other, leaving Jae-ha torn between having to work with her ally Yoo-jin to get revenge and protecting her newfound love An-na from her ally.
