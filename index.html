Descendants of The Sun

By famous dorama

Descendants of the Sun is a 2016 South Korean military drama television series starring Song Joong Ki, Song Hye Kyo, Jin Goo, and Kim Ji Won. It aired on KBS2 from February 24 to April 14, 2016 on Wednesdays and Thursdays at 9:55 pm KST.

Plot

By famous dorama

Yoo Si-jin is the captain of a Korean army special forces unit on a peacekeeping mission in a hotspot in the fictional country of Uruk. A chance meeting with Dr. Kang Mo Young leads to a mutual crush, but their professional activities do not allow the romance to develop smoothly...

Cast

By famous dorama

Song Joong Ki as Captain Yoo Si Jin. Song Hye Kyo as Dr. Kang Mo Young. Jin Goo is Chief Seo Dae Young. Kim Ji Won is an army doctor, Lieutenant Yoon Myung Joo. Kang Shin Eul - Lieutenant General Yoon (father of Yoon Myung Joo).
